FAQs

Common questions

What does "zero-waste" actually mean? +

It means that when our pattern pieces are cut, no fabric is discarded. Every centimetre of the original fabric roll becomes part of one or more garments. No offcuts, no scraps, no landfill.

Are the garments available to purchase? +

Mounam is a portfolio project exploring restriction and self-expression through fashion. Future plans include an e-commerce platform. Contact us if you're interested in commissioning a piece or discussing the concept further.

Can I collaborate with the designer? +

Absolutely. We welcome collaborations with other designers, brands, artists, and sustainability organisations. Use the contact form above and select "Collaboration" as the subject.

Where are the garments made? +

All garments are made in-studio, by hand. Each piece is an individual creation — made with intention, shaped by constraint, finished with care.
